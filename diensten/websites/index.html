What is a CMS system?

Within EctoBytes we have extensive knowledge and expertise about open source content management systems (CMS).

A CMS is a functionality with which the content within, for example, a website can be easily managed. Through a manageable CMS, a user can change texts, add pages and place images on the site, without the help of a website builder or programmer.

We make the CMS and then you are fully in charge of the interpretation of your website.
